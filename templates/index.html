About ZotHacks
ZotHacks is a 12 hour mini-hackathon geared towards beginners. The focus of ZotHacks is to provide people with a gentle introduction into hackathons and crafting meaningful projects. We hope to make hackathons less intimidating and more accessible to newer hackers. The event will be complete with mentorship, helpful workshops, and collaboration.
Core Values

Learn

Learn new technologies and grow your technical skills.

Explore

Explore the hackathon experience and ideas that have impact.

Create

Create meaningful projects that have a real-world benefit.

FAQs

Who is ZotHacks geared towards?

ZotHacks is primarily aimed at people who have never been to a hackathon and want to learn skills that will be useful for future hackathons and meaningful projects.

How are you determining how people will be accepted into ZotHacks?

We will be evaluating application responses and prior experience with hackathons.

How large will teams be and are teams mandatory?

Since collaboration is a strong focus of our event, forming a team is mandatory. Teams will be composed of roughly 4 people each.

How will teams be formed for ZotHacks?

Currently, we are working on the details of team formation and will provide more details once our team finalizes a plan.

How do I know if I have been accepted into ZotHacks?

After the application review process, an email will be sent out to all applicants indicating if they are invited to participate

What can I do to prepare for ZotHacks?

Come in with enthusiasm, a strong desire to learn about technology, and a fully charged laptop and charger.
